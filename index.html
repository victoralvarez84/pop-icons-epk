BIO

Pop Icons' origin story is really just a chain of unsociable drunks meeting each other at different times in Phoenix. First Nate met Rob. Shortly after that, the dynamic duo met Brandon. A few years later Joel joined ranks on lead guitar. In between all of that they began playing songs in Nate's bedroom, then practice spaces, then dive bars and venues around Phoenix. In January of 2020, Pop Icons managed to record a 5 song EP called Prelude that, due to an incredible stroke of luck, came out during a global pandemic. With nothing but time on their hands and living rooms at their disposal, Pop Icons used that time to write new songs that have manifested as a split with Florida's finest, Rutterkin, and a full length called Chasing Lightning. Having played to bigger and bigger crowds, getting positive reviews, from Punknews, Jersey Beat, AZ Central, and The Fear and Loathing Fanzine, and getting a spot on Fest in Gainesville, Pop Icons are looking to bring their blend of East Bay and Midwestern pop punk to the masses.
